Braille-X: Refreshable Mechanical Braille Display

Assistive Technology | Design & Fabrication

  • Designed and 3D-printed a low-cost refreshable Braille mechanism for visually impaired students.
  • Developed a text-to-Braille conversion workflow and optimized tactile readability.
  • Built and tested an electromechanical prototype for repeatable and reliable actuation.

Hybrid Solar–Biomass Thermal System for a Textile Facility

Energy & Environment Course Project

  • Designed a renewable thermal solution combining solar collectors and a biomass boiler for industrial heat demand.
  • Evaluated a stratified thermal storage tank for load-demand matching across production shifts.
  • Performed techno-economic assessment and quantified annual CO₂ reduction potential.

Coal vs. Natural Gas Power Plants with Carbon Capture (CCS)

Power Plant Engineering Sessional Project

  • Performed thermodynamic comparison of coal-fired and gas-turbine plants under varying load conditions.
  • Estimated efficiency penalties and auxiliary power requirements due to CO₂ separation and compression.
  • Evaluated the economic trade-offs using LCOE analysis with and without CCS integration.

Energy Balance of a Solar-Assisted Thermal System

  • Assessed feasibility and payback using realistic demand and system assumptions.
